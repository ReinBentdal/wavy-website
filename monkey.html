MONKEY

Getting started Manual

An ultra-portable wireless music making device

Buy now €59

MONKEY is a two octave Bluetooth MIDI keyboard which literaly fits in your pocket. Bluetooth in MIDI keybaords is nothing new. But ultra long battery life of several months is. This is what enables Bluetooth to actually be practical to use, together with low latency communication.

Through the SHIFT key on MONKEY you get access to all the features written under and above the keys. Monkey has 6 MIDI effects, each with 10 presets. Furthermore it includes a loop recorder for you to record your ideas with up to 10 instruments!

Highlights

  • Several months of battery life. Replaceable CR2032 battery
  • Two octave keybed with low operating force
  • 6 MIDI effects each with 10 presets
  • Loop recorder with up to 10 instruments simultaneously
  • Low latency Bluetooth MIDI
  • Ultra compact form factor of 150mm x 50mm
  • Compatible with many configurations, including macOS, iOS/iPadOS, Android, Windows, CME WIDI and more
