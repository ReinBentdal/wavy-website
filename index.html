Introducing MONKEY

An ultra-portable Bluetooth MIDI keyboard

Monkey at its simplest form is just a Bluetooth MIDI keyboard with ultra long battery life. But through the SHIFT key you get access to all of its other features. Monkey has 6 MIDI effects, each with 10 presets. Furthermore it includes a loop recorder for you to record your ideas with up to 10 instruments!
